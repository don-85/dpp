Privacy Policy

At EmpowerNet, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data remains secure. We adhere to strict data protection standards and comply with all relevant privacy laws to maintain your trust.

What Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and other necessary details to create and manage your account.

Transaction Data: Any donations, payments, or transactions you make on our platform are securely logged to ensure transparency and accountability.

Technical Information: We may collect information about your device, browser, and IP address to enhance the security and functionality of our platform.

How We Use Your Information

Account Management: To manage user accounts, process transactions, and provide personalized services.

Communication: To send you updates, respond to your queries, and notify you about changes to our services.

Security: To detect, prevent, and address any fraudulent activities or breaches.

Your Privacy Is Our Priority

No Unauthorized Sharing: EmpowerNet does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent.

Secure Data Handling: We use advanced encryption and secure servers to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to your information, and they are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

Access and Control: You can review and update your personal information at any time.

Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data from our systems, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

Consent Management: You have the right to manage your communication preferences and opt out of non-essential emails.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies to improve your experience on our platform. Cookies help us:

  • Analyze user behavior to enhance our services.
  • Remember your preferences for a smoother experience.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

To provide a seamless user experience, we may partner with trusted third-party services for payment processing, analytics, or other functionalities. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely for the agreed-upon purposes.

Policy Updates

Our privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

For any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at support@empowernet.com.
